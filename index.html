How Brainstorm Works

Vision & Mission

Vision:

We have built a centralized team within Product Development that has a firm grasp on the rapidly evolving field of artificial intelligence technologies and how we can seamlessly integrate AI with VU processes and applications, transforming the way our internal employees live, work, and connect as well as how our borrowers interact with us.

Mission:

This team will buy into a continuous learning mindset to build a multidisciplinary skillset so we can drive the adoption of AI advancements at VU. In practice, we will:

  • Lead with extreme curiosity
  • Deliver tangible results & own outcomes
  • Collaborate with PS & Architecture
  • Remain decisive in the midst of exploration
  • Build authentic relationships
  • Generate momentum with a bias to action

Core Principles

Innovation Focus: Brainstorm must always retain capacity to drive new projects and priorities.

Long-Term Ownership: Mature products transition to non-Brainstorm teams for sustained ownership.

Brainstorm will likely retain AI capability ownership in the short-to-medium term, with potential to shift ownership to where it makes sense long-term.
